About Me

I am Jenny Sheng, a rising Junior at Princeton University majoring in computer science and minoring in statistics and machine learning. Computer science to me has always been a merging point of creativity and technicality. From a tiny program to a complex web application, computer science brings me into a world of problem solving and bug finding. I find myself tackling all different kinds of puzzles, from the the smallest bugs to the most intricate data structures. There is something beautiful in the formlessness of software. It is nothing, but it is everything, and in every step I take I am reminded that there is still so much for me to learn and explore.
