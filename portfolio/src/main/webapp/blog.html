June 23th, 2019

First Day at Tezign

Hi everyone! I’m Jenny, a freshman BSE cos major, and this is my first blog post! This summer I will be interning with Tezign as a tech intern in product management and R&D, specifically in the AI Marketing PM team. I feel that after a week of work at Tezign it’s about the right time for me to write this journal entry, because things have sort of started to get into a routine, and it's a good place for me to stop and reflect on my experiences so far. I’ve been interested in entrepreneurship and startups for a long time, so I thought that the Princeton Startup Immersion programming would be a great way to experience the startup atmosphere. Besides learning technical skills related to software engineering, I really hope to learn more about what being a software engineer in a startup meant, and some of the product management aspects of a startup. As a cs major there are many different career paths to take, so I see this internship as an opportunity to experience the breadth of paths in startup companies as well as learn more technical skills.

There are many other interns at Tezign as well, and throughout the week I was kind of impressed at the rate that the company is expanding. Almost every other day some new intern or employee would be added to the group chat. I also attended one of the product management meetings, and it was my first company meeting at Tezign. During the meeting I was surprised that although the manager who was leading the meeting spoke regularly, she did not mind at all when some other coworker cut her off to express their ideas. Everyone was freely discussing and sharing their ideas.

I arrived a week before they were about to move location. Tezign is at max capacity and about to move to a newer, bigger location. But even though this would mean that I would have a longer commute, I look forward to spending the rest of my summer at Tezign.
